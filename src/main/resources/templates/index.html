The aim of this dissertation is to create an application that can be utilised by you, a musician to both calculate your emissions and allow you to calculate offsets.

It will promote sustainability by leading by example in your target market of people in the age range 15-35.

The whole world is trying to mitigate climate change and soon it will be expected of musicians.

This application will help you as musicians make your concerts more sustainable thus joining the world in mitigating climate change.
